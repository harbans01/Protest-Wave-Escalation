Replicator Dynamics Model:
  dx/dt = x(1-x)[Bx - C]

  x = fraction participating
  B = benefit of protesting
  C = cost of protesting

Key insight: Early growth rate (dx/dt) predicts whether the wave escalates

Your Features Operationalize This:
  • onset_growth = dx/dt at t=0 (initial momentum)
  • onset_acceleration = d²x/dt² at t=0 (trajectory change)
  • These are observable in FIRST 2 WEEKS (no future info leakage!)
  • Random Forest model learns to predict escalation based on these

Why It Works:
